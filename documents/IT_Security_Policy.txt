# Acme Corp IT Security Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

## 1. Password Requirements
All employee accounts must use passwords that are at least 14 characters long. Passwords must contain a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Passwords expire every 90 days and cannot be reused within a 12-month period.

## 2. Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
MFA is mandatory for all company systems. Employees must use the AcmeAuthenticator mobile app. SMS-based MFA is strictly prohibited due to security vulnerabilities.

## 3. Data Classification
Company data is classified into three tiers:
- **Public**: Marketing materials, press releases.
- **Internal**: Employee handbooks, org charts.
- **Confidential**: Customer data, financial records, source code. 

Confidential data must never be stored on local machine hard drives or external USB drives. It must remain on encrypted cloud servers (AcmeCloud).

## 4. Incident Reporting
If you suspect a phishing attempt, a data breach, or if you lose your company laptop, you must immediately call the 24/7 IT Emergency Hotline at 1-800-555-0199 or email security-incident@acmecorp.com. Do not attempt to fix the issue yourself.
