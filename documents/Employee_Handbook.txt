# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Acme Corp! This handbook outlines our company policies, benefits, and code of conduct. 

## 2. Remote Work Policy
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days a week (Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday). Mondays and Fridays are mandatory in-office days to foster team collaboration. All remote work must be conducted within the country of employment for tax purposes.

## 3. Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of PTO per calendar year. Up to 5 unused PTO days can be rolled over to the next year. Sick leave is separate and unlimited, provided a doctor's note is submitted for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

## 4. Hardware Allowance
Upon joining, every employee is granted a $1,000 stipend to set up their home office. This stipend refreshes every 3 years. Receipts must be submitted via the internal expense portal (AcmeSpend) within 30 days of purchase.

## 5. Contact Information
For HR-related inquiries, please contact hr@acmecorp.com. For payroll issues, contact payroll@acmecorp.com.
